Welcome to the Embed Tester Store

To install a module, simply add the following to your QuickCSS, just below Flashcord's @import.

@import url("https://sirio-network.com/flashcord/sid.css")

/* Add the Import line of the module you want just below here */

@import url("https://sirio-network.com/flashcord/store/module_name/module.css")

The naming format is always in lower case, and spaces are replaced by underscores.

For example: "Extended Chat Effects" becomes "extended_chat_effects".

To know more about a module, simply click its name.
